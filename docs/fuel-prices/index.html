FAQ

Why is diesel different across provinces?
Provincial fuel taxes drive most of the gap. BC adds carbon tax and transit levies. Alberta has lower fuel levies and no provincial sales tax. Transportation to remote areas also pushes prices higher. The AB-NL spread is 23.6¢/L — $94 on a 400-litre fill.
Where should I fuel up?
Alberta has the lowest prices. Saskatchewan and Manitoba are next. Fill up before leaving Alberta or Saskatchewan if heading east or west — prices jump at the provincial borders.
How often do prices update?
Every 30 minutes from public fuel surveys across all ten provinces.
Do these include carbon tax?
Yes. All federal and provincial taxes are included.
