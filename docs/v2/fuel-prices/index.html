FAQ

Why is diesel so different across provinces?
Provincial fuel taxes drive most of the gap. BC adds carbon tax, transit levies, and a dedicated fuel tax. Alberta has no provincial sales tax and lower fuel levies. Transportation costs to remote areas push prices higher too. Right now the spread between AB and NL is 23.6¢/L — that's $94 on a 400-litre fill.
Where should I fuel to save money?
Alberta has the lowest prices. Saskatchewan and Manitoba are next. If you're running cross-country, fill up before leaving Alberta or Saskatchewan — prices jump significantly east of Manitoba and west of the Rockies.
How often do prices update?
Every 30 minutes from public fuel surveys across all ten provinces.
Do these prices include carbon tax?
Yes. All federal and provincial taxes are included in the prices shown.
